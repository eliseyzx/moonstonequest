Storyline 1

Welcome, Heritage Explorer.

Years ago, Moonstone Estate was one of Singapore's vibrant neighbourhoods, filled with bustling streets, family-run businesses and close-knit communities. Your mission is to recover six lost pieces of its history and restore its forgotten legacy.

  1. 2

    Booth 2 · Gemstone Estate Builder

    Restore the damaged map

    An old map from the National Archives has been damaged over time. Piece together the neighbourhood before its story can be told.

    Challenge: Estate Cartographer

    Assemble puzzle pieces for Moonstone Lane, Ruby Lane, Opal Crescent and Topaz Road to rebuild the estate map. Complete it in under 40 seconds to earn three times the points.

    Learning outcome: See how the gemstone streets connect and understand the estate layout.

    Digital badge · Estate Cartographer

    Excellent work—you have restored the map and can now see how the Gemstone Streets form this historic neighbourhood.

  2. 3

    Booth 3 · Street Name Puzzle

    Match the streets to their heritage clues

    With the map restored, discover why the streets are named after gemstones and what makes this neighbourhood distinctive.

    Challenge: Gemstone Street Match

    Match the street cards—Moonstone Lane, Ruby Lane, Opal Crescent and Topaz Road—to heritage fact cards: gemstone naming, Moonstone Estate, the Woodsville/Kallang area and the Gemstone Streets identity.

    Reward · Get every match right in one try to receive a 2× Ruby token.

    The roads of Moonstone Estate follow a gemstone naming theme, giving the area its enduring nickname: the Gemstone Streets.

  3. 5

    Booth 5 · Moonstone Marketplace

    Bring the marketplace back to life

    Once-quiet streets awaken with the aroma of coffee, a tailor at work, bicycles being repaired and neighbours collecting daily essentials.

    Challenge: Marketplace Match

    Take a shopping list and match each item to the traditional business that served the community: rice to the provision shop, fabric to the tailor, coffee powder to the coffee shop and bicycle repair to the bicycle repair shop.

    Learning outcome: Learn how small businesses supported everyday life in Moonstone Estate.

    Reward · Topaz Token

    Small businesses were the heart of Moonstone Estate—creating places for neighbours to meet, interact and build community ties.

  4. 6

    Booth 6 · Then & Now

    Compare the past and present

    Moonstone Estate has transformed alongside Singapore's development. Some familiar landmarks have disappeared, while others remain as reminders of the past.

    Challenge: Then & Now

    Compare an old photograph with a current photograph. Identify what stayed the same and what changed, then reflect on why conservation matters.

    Learning outcome: Understand how preserving neighbourhood heritage keeps stories, communities and traditions alive for the future.

    Reward · Diamond Token
