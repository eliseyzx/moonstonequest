How to get there

Choose your route.

Allow a little extra time for the final walk and check the live journey planner before travelling—services and stop arrangements can change.

🚇

By MRT

Potong Pasir MRT

North East Line · NE10

  1. Exit Potong Pasir MRT and use a live map for the pedestrian route towards Moonstone Lane and Topaz Road.
  2. Continue towards the PIE footbridge at Topaz Road.
  3. Enter via the signed Topaz Road visitor entrance and follow Moonstone Quest signs to the booths.

🚶 Check the live route for current walking time and distance.

🚌

By bus

Serangoon Road / Woodsville area

Use the nearest live-listed stop for Topaz Road

  1. Use the LTA or Google Maps journey planner to select a current bus service and stop near Topaz Road.
  2. Walk towards the PIE footbridge and Topaz Road.
  3. Use the signed visitor entrance; the pop-up booths are inside the estate below the PIE.

🚶 Confirm stop number, services, walking time and distance in the live planner before departure.

Transport information is deliberately linked to live journey planners rather than fixed service listings, which can change. Open LTA public transport information ↗
